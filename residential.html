DEFY THE ALBERTA ELEMENTS.

From -40°C freezes to scorching July UV. Your property takes a beating. We restore it.

  • 01.

    THE FREEZE / THAW CYCLE

    In Edmonton, moisture seeps into concrete pores, freezes, and expands—causing spalling and cracks. Our deep-clean and seal process locks out water, protecting your exposed aggregate and stamped concrete from the next winter cycle.

  • 02.

    CORROSIVE ROAD SALTS

    City plows track magnesium chloride onto your driveway, eating away the surface and pitting your pavers. We use hot water vapor blasting to extract these salts from the substrate before permanent damage sets in.

  • 03.

    NORTH-FACE ALGAE & MOSS

    Shaded walkways in St. Albert and Sherwood Park are breeding grounds for slippery green algae. Our soft-wash system kills the spores at the root—restoring grip and appearance without using harsh chemicals that kill your lawn.

  • 04.

    UV OXIDATION & FADE

    High-altitude sun turns cedar decks gray and makes vinyl siding chalky. We strip away the dead fibers and oxidized layers using organic corn cob media, prepping your wood and siding for a fresh, vibrant finish.
