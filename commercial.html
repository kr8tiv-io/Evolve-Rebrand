PRESERVING THE GRAIN.

Log homes are a massive investment. Traditional sandblasting destroys the soft wood grain, leaving a pitted, "fuzzy" finish that ruins the aesthetic.

We use organic media (Crushed Walnut / Corn Cob) to gently strip failing stain and UV mill glaze without eroding the wood itself.

  • Lodges & Resorts: Large scale restoration with minimal guest disruption.
  • Timber Frame: Precision cleaning for intricate joinery and beams.
  • No Water Damage: Dry or ultra-low moisture options for sensitive wood.

ORGANIC MEDIA

Biodegradable corn & walnut shells absorb coatings rather than cutting the substrate.

GRAIN PRESERVATION

Eliminates pitting and wood fuzzing, leaving the natural substrate profile intact and smooth.

STAIN-READY SURFACE

A completely dry process means zero waiting time for moisture evaporation. You can begin coating immediately.
