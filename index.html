THE ECO-ADVANTAGE

Our technology adapts the media to the substrate.

Recycled Glass

100% inert and non-toxic. Sharp enough for rust, safe for soil and plants.

Walnut & Corn

Biodegradable organic media. Absorbs coatings rather than cutting the substrate.

Hot Water Vapor

Adds thermal energy to melt grease instantly while suppressing 92% of dust.

Safety Compliance

Low dust methods significantly improve OH&S compliance compared to dry silica.

No Chemical Runoff

The media remains inert, allowing for easier containment and disposal without harmful chemical wastewater runoff.

Restores PH Balance

Our process is safer for landscaping and prevents the harsh pH changes typical of chemical strippers.

Minimal Waste

Less material usage overall due to efficient cleaning power, reducing environmental footprint.

Non-Sparking Media

Use of specialty non-ferrous media for projects near active systems where spark mitigation is essential.

Reduced Containment

Because our media is wet, it drops to the ground immediately, requiring significantly less plastic tenting.

Silica-Free Certification

We provide full documentation ensuring your job site remains free of harmful, carcinogenic silica dust.
